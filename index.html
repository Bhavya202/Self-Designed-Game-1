Game Design Document

What is the title of your game?

The SpaceShip: Three Parts

What is the goal of your game?

The goal of the game is to renovate the spaceship by finding the three parts of the spaceship.

Write a brief story of the game?

The story of the game is like that the spaceship has been crashed with an meteroid and now, you have to repair it by fixing it's three parts by visiting three planets and completing the three tasks and repair the spaceship with the new parts.

What are the playing characters of the game?

Number Character Name What will this character do?
1 Space Ship It will go into planets, complete tasks and arrange the parts for renovating it.

What are the non-playing characters of the game?

Number Character Name What will this character do?
1 Planets It have task which the user have to do to find the parts.
2 Tasks They are the game that the user have to play to find the parts.
